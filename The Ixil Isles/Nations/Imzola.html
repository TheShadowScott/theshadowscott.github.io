Duchy of Imzola

* This is a subset of the crimes against the person act, which was ratified meaning any crime involving depriving a person of personal liberty. Crimes most obscene typically refers to crimes of a sexual nature, but can also refer to other crimes that are considered to be particularly heinous.

History

Whilst Azuria is known for its production of white mages, Imzola is known for its (sometimes infamous) Deftol college, the worlds most prestigious institution for combat mages, warlocks and necromancers. The questionable antics of the college's studies have weighed heavily on the nation's criminal code, and unlike many nations Imzola had a very lax stance on the use of necromancy and other "crimes against the person". Importantly, however, the worst of these crimes were ratified in a special act, in which they are called "crimes most obscene".

The Duke of Imzola is the head of state, and also the head of Deftol's board of governors. Most day-to-day governance (including that of the entire country) is handled by said board of governors, and the Duke is largely a figurehead, although he retains some real power.

Imzola is represented by the magician in the game of cards.
