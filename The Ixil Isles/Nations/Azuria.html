The Azurian Kingdom

History

The nation of Azuria was founded by King Alford I, an exile from the nation of Imzola in the War of Blood. The nation was founded on the 7th Sun, 30th year of the Quicksilver Age of the Hierophant Era. Until the Grand Inquisition, the nation was a monarchy (headed by a King regent), however post-inquisition, the head of government is the head of the Church of Glory (also known as the Glorious Viceroy). The monarch still serves as ceremonial head of state, but their title has been reduced from King to Prince. Although other religions were outlawed in the Bladed Era, the laws were repealed in the Healing.

Since the repeal of the laws, the Viceroy, in an effort to repair ill-will between the Azurian Kingdom and other religions, founded the Golden Chalice, a guild that spans across the Ixil Isles. The guild is dedicated to the preservation of faith and the protection of religious freedom (with the notable exception of the diabolic faith, which both the Azurian Kingdom and the Golden Chalice are dedicated to eradicating).

Azuria is represented by the high priestess in the game of cards.
