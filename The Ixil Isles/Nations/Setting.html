Kingdom of Setting

* Crimes against the person was ratified meaning any crime involving depriving a person of personal liberty. This definition is broad and includes kidnapping, false imprisonment, human trafficking, &c.

History

One of many nations to have split from Helstedge, the Kingdom of Setting has become a powerful player in its own right with the worlds strongest functioning navy. With that navy, Setting also has the worlds largest merchant fleet, and boasts the largest maritime economy.

The name "Kingdom" in Settings name is misleading, as the nation is a republic. The name is a relic from the nations founding, when the nation was a monarchy, formerly headed by the Archduke of Setting (the viceroy of Helstedge). The nations splitting gave rise to the name of the Setting age, which followed shortly after this nations founding.

Setting is represented by the hanged man in the game of cards.
