Eras & Ages of The Ixil Isles

The major time periods of the Ixil Isles are called Eras, time spans equating to 1,250 years. Each era is divided into five Ages, each lasting 250 years. The last year of the final age of an era is called a Turning, and is often marked by significant events, specifically the game of cards (which technially takes place over two and a half years), an event which determines the next era's head-of-council (the lead member of the crowned council). There have been six eras to date, and the seventh era is due to occur in three years. The following are the recorded ages of the Ixil Isles:
