Deftol College

Basic Information

Deftol College
Allowed Classes Bards, Sorcerers (all), Warlocks (all except celestial), Wizards (all)
Allowed Races Any
Leader Rectoress Cyntia Vermilion
Alignment True Neutral, accepts any

Deftol is the less "ideal" of the two mage colleges, but is often regarded as the most practical. To a mage of Deftol, the ends justify the means, and there is nothing, nothing that can rightfully stop one in the pursuit of knowledge. Deftol is located in Krython, Imzola.
