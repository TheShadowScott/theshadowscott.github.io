Basic Information

Thieve's Guild
Allowed Classes All

Leader The Four Cloaks (azure, gules, vert and Or)
Alignment Neutral Evil, accepts any non-lawful, non-good alignment

The Thieve's Guild is a guild of rogues, thieves, and other ne'er-do-wells. The guild is based in the city of Argentum, located in Azuria. To most members (and almost all of the outside world), the leaders of this guild are only known by the colour of the cloak they wear, although the higher ranking members of the guild are required to meet with the leaders to promote.
