The Golden Chalice

Basic Information

Golden Chalice
Allowed Classes All
Allowed Races All, although certain races may be suspected
Leader Viceroy Maximilian Purdue
Alignment Lawful Good, accepts any non-evil alignment

The Golden Chalice is a guild of clerics, paladins, and other devotees, spanning across all non-heretical faiths. The officer warrants of the Golden Chalice are special, in that they are appointed outside of the guild, by their respective gods. The guild is based in the city of Argentum, located in Azuria.
