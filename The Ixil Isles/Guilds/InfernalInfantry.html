Infernal Infantry

Basic Information

Infernal Infantry
Allowed Classes Any, except non-oathbreaker paladins and clerics*
Allowed Races Any, except Aasmir
Leader Asmodeus
Alignment Lawful Evil, accepts any non-good alignment

The Infernal Infantry is a guild of soldiers bound to the will of the Archdevil Asmodeus. The guild is based in Nessus, the deepest and most dangerous layer of the Nine Hells.
* Clerics of the diabolic faith are welcome in the Infernal Infantry
