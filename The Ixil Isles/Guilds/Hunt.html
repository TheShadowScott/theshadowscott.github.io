The Great Hunt

Basic Information

Great Hunt
Allowed Classes Barbarian, Druid, Fighter, Ranger
Allowed Races All, although certain races may be suspected
Leader Archdruid Miðlo Krøk
Alignment Lawful Good, accepts any non-evil alignment

The great hunt is a guild of hunters, rangers, and other folk that have a strong tie to nature. The guild is based in the sprawling Immol forests, located in between the nations of Imzola and Elydius.
