Chancellor's College

Basic Information

Chancellor's College
Allowed Classes Sorcerers (Divine, Lunar, Wild); Warlock (Celestial); Wizards (Abjuration, Divination, Enchantment, Illusion, Transmutation)
Allowed Races Aasmir, Elves, Half-Elves, Halflings, Humans
Leader The Lord Chancellor Errastos
Alignment Lawful Good, accepts any non-evil, non-chaotic alignment

Often regarded as the "pure" mages college, Chancellor's College is a place of learning primarily for non-destructive magic. The college is based in the city of Elysium, the capital of the Nation of Elydius.
