Ixil Isles Campaign Manager

Guilds and Factions

Throughout this campaign, players will have the opportunity to join various guilds and factions. Players may use downtime granted at the end of each session to advance their standing within their faction, in addition to completing quests during gameplay. Performing quests (and using downtime) will grant players guild crests which can be used to purchase items, services and other benefits from their faction. Once a player has acquired a certain amount of guild crests, they may be eligible for a promotion-of-rank. Each player may only be a member of one faction at a time.

Promotions & Ranks

Each faction has its own set of ranks and promotion requirements. At lower ranks, players will only be required to collect a certain amount of guild crests to be eligible for a promotion. At higher ranks, however, players will be required to complete a quest or series of quests to be eligible for a promotion. Ranks will grant players access to unique items and benefits, as well as unique and innate abilities.

Ranks are split into three categories: Associate, Member and Officer. Progressing between categories requires a guild warrant. Specifics on how to obtain a guild warrant will be detailed in the faction's page, and each subsequent warrant will become increasingly difficult to obtain.
